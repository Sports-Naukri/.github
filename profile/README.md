# Welcome to **SportsNaukri**!  
Your sport. Your career. Your move.

## What is SportsNaukri?

*SportsNaukri* is India’s premier platform dedicated to connecting passionate sports professionals with top opportunities across the sports industry. Whether you’re looking to start or grow your career in sports, or you’re an employer seeking the best talent, SportsNaukri makes it happen.

## Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between sports professionals and employers, empowering individuals to build rewarding careers and helping organizations thrive with the right talent.

## What We Offer

### For Candidates
- Discover the latest sports jobs across India.
- Apply for positions such as Physio, Trainer, Sports Centre Head, Sponsorship roles, and more.
- Manage your profile and applications with an intuitive dashboard.
- Receive job alerts and bookmark your favorite opportunities.
- Upload your CV and step up to make your mark in the world of sports.

### For Employers
- Access a pool of pre-screened sports professionals.
- Browse candidate profiles and shortlist potential hires.
- Post jobs and manage listings through our Employer Dashboard.
- Choose from flexible recruitment packages to suit your hiring needs.
